StreetLink

StreetLink is a non-profit charity organisation exists to help end rough sleeping, by enabling members of the public to connect people sleeping rough with the local services that can support them.

Areas of expertise

  • Accommodation
  • Housing First
  • Improving homelessness services
  • Meeting diverse needs
  • Rough sleeping
  • Understanding homelessness

Our Vision and Mission

Our vision is to truckle homelessness, as we believe that everyone should have a place to call home and the support they need to keep it. And our goal is to develop, inspire, support, and sustain a movement of organisations working together to achieve positive futures for people who are homeless or vulnerably housed.
